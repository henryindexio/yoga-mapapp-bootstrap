Open a Yoga Studio

Yogee is a web app that recommends good neighborhoods to open new yoga studios in NY state. Aspiring yoga studio owners can type in the zipcode where they want open a store and Yogee shows nearby districts that are good locations. The Yogee score (0-100) is the predicted chance that a studio opening at that location can survive at least one year.
