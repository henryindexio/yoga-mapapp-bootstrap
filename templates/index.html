Open a Yoga Studio

Yogee is a web app that recommends good neighborhoods to open new yoga studios in NY state. Aspiring yoga studio owners can type in the zip code where they want open a store and Yogee shows 5 nearby zip codes that are good locations. The Yogee score (0-100) is the predicted percent chance that a studio opening at that location can survive at least one year. Yogee shows the demographics data for the recommended zip codes so users can determine which location has the customers they want to target.
